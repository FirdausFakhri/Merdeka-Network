About Merdeka Network

Established on 22 August 2022, Merdeka Network Sdn Bhd is a forward-thinking Malaysian company dedicated to excellence and innovation across multiple sectors. We specialize in the construction of engineering projects, telecommunication reselling, and the wholesale distribution of a diverse range of goods.

Our core strength lies in integrating cutting-edge technology with industry best practices, ensuring every project is executed with precision and a keen eye for detail. We believe in the power of collaboration and building long-lasting relationships through exceptional service.
